Turtles: Quiet, Ancient, and Fascinating

About Turtles

Turtles are reptiles. They are known for their hard shells, slow and steady movement, and long lifespans. Many species are excellent swimmers while others roam on land. People all over the world appreciate turtles for their calm presence and important ecological roles.

Quick Turtle Facts

  • Turtles have existed for over 200 million years.
  • Some species migrate long distances.
  • They play important roles in their ecosystems.
